Harare Chamber of Commerce

Purpose:

  • The purpose of the Harare Chamber of Commerce is to promote tourism in Zimbabwe allowing for economic growth and development of the country.
  • Another purpose is for the empowerment of the youth by helping the start self sustaining projects and engaging the in various activities with the aim to combat drug abuse.
